# Feature: Course Management

## Overview
Enables users to create, organize, and publish courses with approval and fee logic.

## Details
- Courses can be free or paid; paid courses require approval charge.
- Approval by Admin/Superadmin required before publishing.
- Course type (free/paid) is locked after approval.
- Only author, Admin, or Superadmin can modify courses before approval.
- Suggestions: Support course modules/sections, prerequisites, and completion certificates.

## Supported Content Types
- **PDFs**: Upload and embed PDF documents as course material.
- **Videos**: Upload or link to video lessons within courses.
- **Quizzes**: Integrate quizzes as part of the course flow for assessment and engagement.
